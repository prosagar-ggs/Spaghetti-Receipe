Spaghetti Recipe

  • Ingredients
  • Instructions

    • 1.Ingredients

    • Spaghetti
    • Marina Sause
    • Salt
    • Water

    2.Instructions

    1. Bring water to a boil.
    2. Add spaghetti to boiling water.
      1. Add salt to taste.
      2. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    3. Add marinara sauce to a pan, bringing to a simmer.
    4. Mix cooked spaghetti with marinara sauce.

    Enjoy!
